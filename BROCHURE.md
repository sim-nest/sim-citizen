# sim-citizen

In one line: how a domain's own data types become first-class, well-behaved SIM values, with the wiring written for you.

## What it gives you

A one-line marker that generates everything needed to make your Rust type a full SIM value, and the layer that lets those types show up across codecs, dispatch, and views like anything native to the system -- no special cases, no hand-written glue.
